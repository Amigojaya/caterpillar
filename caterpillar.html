What i observed about Caterpillar Eats

Loves to Eat

  • Anjur leaves
  • Mexican sunflower leaves
  • Okra Leaves (Ladies finger)
  • Custard Apple flowers
  • Tecoma stans flowers (Pasupu puvullu)
  • Tecoma alata flowers (orange bell flowers)

Hates to Eat

  • Custard apple leaves
  • Guava leaves
  • Hibiscus leaves
  • Papaya leaves
  • Goose-berry leaves
  • Ixora Leaves( nuru varahalu )
These are the things i observed
-> Any flowers can be eaten by Caterpillers, literally any Flower
-> They like only thin leaves or flowers to eat
